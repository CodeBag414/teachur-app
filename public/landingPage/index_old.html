How Does it Work?

Objectives, lessons, courses and degrees ... sound familiar?

Teachurs was built by teachers and instructional designers. We speak the language and know the process.

Like all good instructional design, the foundation of Teachur is the objective. We help you write world class objectives with explicit outcomes and clear assesments. Or you can look through our community created database to find examples to get you started.

But design isn't only about the objective. Teachur helps you organize your objectives into lessons, your lessons into courses and even your courses into their own aligned degrees.

Collaboration

Work with others down the hall or around the world

Design is better with input and collaboration. Teachur allows you to share your work with others in your field, get feedback, and easily integrate that feedback into your design.

Whether you are working with others in your department to create a perfectly designed degree, someone on the other side of campus to create integrated studies, or someone around the world to get feedback and discuss ideas on how to teach in your field, Teachur makes the process easy and productive.

About Us

Who we are

We are a group of teachers, developers and instructional designers. We are interested in changing and improving instructional design in higher ed.


Teachur was created because it was a tool that we wanted to use. Having worked in higher ed for decades, we saw the need for something that would not only cut down on the time it took to design course content, but also improve the quality.


At Teachur we want to improve what you do. Have a suggestion or idea that would improve Teachur? Email us at contact@teachur.co
